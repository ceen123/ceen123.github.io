Strength

Wraith King

Wraith King sears an enemy unit with spectral fire, dealing damage and stunning, then dealing damage over time and slowing the target's attack and movement speed. If the target dies while under this effect, they will become a ghost and attack their former allies. Wraith King can gain an extra skeleton charge during Mortal Strike, and his skeletons can apply a stacking attack slow to enemy units.

Universal

Windranger

Evasive and swift, the nimble Windranger is always seeking the right angle to attack. Whether she's clearing the way with a draw of her deadly powershot, or shackling enemies together to pepper them with arrows, she's always on the move.
